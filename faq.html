Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most from Big Island homeowners.

Most residential re-roofing projects take 1–3 days depending on roof size and complexity. We provide a specific timeline in your estimate and keep you updated daily. We work efficiently to minimize the disruption to your household.
Yes. We have extensive experience working with all major insurance companies operating in Hawaii. We can document damage with photos and reports, prepare estimates in insurance-standard formats, and in many cases communicate directly with adjusters on your behalf.
For Hilo's extreme rainfall and humidity, we most often recommend metal roofing (standing seam or corrugated) for its longevity and water-shedding performance, or high-quality architectural shingles with enhanced algae and wind resistance. We'll recommend the best material for your specific home, budget, and HOA requirements.
Absolutely. We hold a valid Hawaii State Contractor's License (BC-23891), carry $2 million in general liability insurance, and maintain full workers' compensation coverage for all employees. We can provide certificates of insurance upon request.
Roofing costs vary based on roof size, pitch, material selection, and existing condition. Asphalt shingle re-roofing typically runs $8,500–$18,000 for an average home. Metal roofing runs $14,000–$35,000. We provide free, detailed written estimates so you know exactly what you're getting before any work begins.
All new roof installations come with a 10-year workmanship warranty covering any installation defects. Additionally, the roofing materials themselves carry manufacturer warranties ranging from 25 to lifetime depending on the product chosen. We're GAF Master Elite certified, which means we can offer enhanced system warranties up to 50 years.
Yes. We offer 24/7 emergency response for active leaks and storm damage. Call (808) 555-0111 any time. In severe weather situations, we prioritize emergency tarping to prevent further water damage while we assess and schedule permanent repairs.
You don't need to be present for most of the work — our team is trustworthy and professional. However, we do ask that someone is available for the start-of-day walkthrough and end-of-day inspection. We'll coordinate with you to make this as convenient as possible.
Our free inspection will tell you definitively. General guidelines: if your roof is under 15 years old with localized damage, repairs usually make sense. If it's over 20 years old, has widespread issues, or has had multiple repairs, replacement typically offers better long-term value. We'll always give you an honest recommendation.
We serve the entire Big Island including Kea'au, Pahoa, Mountain View, Volcano, Waimea, and occasionally Kona. Travel fees may apply for destinations more than 40 miles from Hilo. Call us to discuss your project location.
