About Summit Roofing Hawaii

Two decades of protecting Big Island homes and businesses from Hilo's relentless weather.

Our Story

Built from the Ground Up

Summit Roofing Hawaii was founded in 2004 by Marcus Kahanamoku, a third-generation Hawaii Island carpenter who watched too many friends lose their roofs — and their savings — to contractors who didn't understand the unique demands of island climate.

Marcus started with a truck, a ladder, and a phone number. Twenty years later, Summit employs 18 full-time roofing professionals, operates three fully-equipped trucks, and has completed over 1,400 roofing projects across the Big Island.

Our mission remains the same: give every homeowner and business owner the honest assessment, quality materials, and skilled installation their roof deserves. No upselling. No cutting corners. Just work that lasts.
